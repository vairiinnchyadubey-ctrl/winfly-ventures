Approach · 05 Steps

The brief is the brief. The room is the room. Everything in between is our work.

We do not subcontract judgment. Every mandate is led by a senior partner from first call to final invoice.

  • 01

    Mandate

    We listen first. To the brief, to the unsaid, to the political and reputational stakes that frame it.

  • 02

    Architecture

    A document — narrative, not a deck. Venue, minute-by-minute programme, risk register, escalation matrix.

  • 03

    Production

    Design, fabrication, technology, hospitality. Built to a standard that survives an unannounced visit at 0500.

  • 04

    Show

    The day itself. Dual control rooms, one principal point of contact, no surprises that have not been rehearsed.

  • 05

    Reckoning

    Within seven days: a written debrief, a clean financial close, an honest list of what we would do differently.
